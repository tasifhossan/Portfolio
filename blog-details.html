My Favorite UI/UX Design Trends This Year

This year, UI/UX design has embraced minimalism with purpose—clean layouts paired with bold typography are taking the spotlight. Micro-interactions have become more refined, enhancing user engagement without distraction. Dark mode continues to rise, offering both aesthetic appeal and user comfort. Neumorphism & glassmorphism are being used more selectively to create depth without overwhelming the interface.
